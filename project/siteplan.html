Helping Hands Foundation Site Plan

Site Name

Helping Hands Foundation

This name reflects the mission of the organization to provide support, healthcare services, education, and hope to vulnerable individuals, especially the elderly, low-income families, and young girls.

Optional Domain: helpinghandsfoundation

Site Purpose

The Helping Hands Foundation website serves as an online platform dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable individuals through medical outreaches, health education, free medication distribution, and safety awareness programs for young girls.

The website will:

  • Provide information about outreach programs.
  • Share health education resources.
  • Promote child protection and safety awareness.
  • Allow visitors to volunteer or contact the foundation.
  • Showcase community impact stories and events.

Scenarios

  • How can I volunteer for upcoming medical outreach programs?
  • What health education resources are available for young girls and elderly individuals?
  • How can I support or donate to the foundation's activities?
  • Where can I find information about upcoming community events?

Color Schema

Deep Blue (#1E3A5F) - Used for headings, navigation, and footer.
Soft Green (#4CAF50) - Used for buttons, highlights, and call-to-action areas.
Light Gray (#F4F4F4) - Used for section backgrounds.

Typography

Poppins - Used for headings and navigation.

Open Sans - Used for body text and general content.

Arial - Fallback font.
